# Genie in a Test Tube -- Art Asset List

**Project:** Visual Novel (Unity, 1920x1080 target resolution)
**Style:** TBD (discuss with artist)
**Format:** PNG with transparency for sprites/CGs. Backgrounds can be opaque.

---

## Setting

Near-future sci-fi (2068). Advanced tech (holographics, hoverchairs, nanites) but grounded -- no aliens, no magic. Think upgraded present day. The story draws heavily from 1001 Nights / Aladdin symbolism, which can subtly inform design choices (lamp motifs, Arabian-inspired patterns, etc.).

---

## 1. Character Sprites

All sprites should be drawn at a consistent scale/style. Characters appear side-by-side in dialogue scenes (up to 3 on screen at once). Sprites need transparent backgrounds.

### Hanna Shazi (Protagonist) -- Wheelchair Form (Act 1)

- **Description:** Late 30s woman. Red hair (straight), green eyes, fair skin. Seated in a wheelchair. Military bearing despite the chair -- she carries herself with defiance.
- **Base Pose:** 1 (seated in wheelchair, facing slightly toward camera)
- **Expressions needed (7):**
  - Neutral / guarded
  - Suspicious (slitted eyes)
  - Angry (bared teeth or clenched jaw)
  - Smirking (sarcastic half-smile)
  - Tearful (eyes wet, vulnerable)
  - Pain (grimacing, squeezing eyes shut) -- used in Act 2
  - Tender / maternal (soft smile, eyes warm)

### Mataleli "Mat" Galanidi (Antagonist) -- Office Attire (Act 1)

- **Description:** 50s-60s man. Deep sepia skin, closely cropped curly black hair (receding), amber eyes, thick horn-rimmed glasses. Silver suit with paisley embroidery. Holographic merlot-colored tie (the tie displays an animated horse -- can be a static stylized horse pattern for the sprite).
- **Base Poses:** 2
  1. Seated at desk / standing (default office pose)
  2. Kneeling beside Hanna (emotional moment, Scene 1.6)
- **Expressions needed (6):**
  - Composed smile (mask of charm)
  - Sympathetic (genuine sadness showing through)
  - Emotional (moved, eyes glistening)
  - Shocked (mouth open, glasses slipping)
  - Enraged (dangerous, mask fully off)
  - Steepled fingers pose (if feasible as expression variant)

### Mataleli "Mat" Galanidi -- Surgical Scrubs (Act 2)

- **Description:** Same character, now wearing surgical scrubs instead of the silver suit. Hair may be covered. Glasses still on.
- **Base Pose:** 1 (standing)
- **Expressions needed (3):**
  - Composed (in control)
  - Shocked (the wrong baby)
  - Enraged (violent, grabbing for scalpel)

### Centaur Nurse (Supporting) -- Act 2

- **Description:** Woman's upper body in surgical scrubs, lower body is a spotted white-and-brown horse. Blue eyes. Thick tail. She is strong, protective, fierce when needed.
- **Base Pose:** 1 (full centaur body visible)
- **Expressions needed (2):**
  - Calm / professional
  - Protective / fierce ("You will not hurt this child")

### Centaur Doctor (Supporting) -- Act 2

- **Description:** Man's upper body in surgical scrubs and face mask (mask can be pulled down for expressions), lower body is a brown horse.
- **Base Pose:** 1 (full centaur body visible)
- **Expressions needed (2):**
  - Calm / professional
  - Concerned / urgent ("Uh, Sir. You need to see this")

A large blue fabric curtain divides the room at roughly the midpoint, blocking the view of the lower half. Surgical trays with metal tools visible. Monitors with vital readouts. - **Variants:** 1

## 3. CG Illustrations (6 event images)

Full-scene illustrations for key story moments. These replace the normal sprite + background view. Should be the most polished pieces.

### CG 1: Holographic Horse (Scene 1.1)

- **Description:** A small holographic horse leaps from Mat's necktie, gallops across the lapel of his silver suit, and jumps into a framed picture of a red barn on the wall behind him. The horse glows merlot/wine-colored. Mat watches it with a fond, practiced amusement.
- **Mood:** Wondrous, slightly unsettling (too magical for an office)

### CG 2: Daughter's Photo (Scene 1.6)

- **Description:** A holographic photograph floating above Mat's desk. It shows a little blonde girl (around 5-6) in a princess costume, sitting in a hoverchair decorated with pink fairy wings. She is looking through a wooden fence at horses in a field. Her expression is pure joy. The photo has a faint holographic shimmer/border.
- **Mood:** Bittersweet, nostalgic, heartbreaking

### CG 3: The Touchpad (Scene 1.8)

- **Description:** Close-up of Hanna's hand pressing down on a smooth black touchpad device on Mat's desk. A green light pulses from beneath her palm. Her eyes are half-closed, already losing consciousness. The edges of the image are starting to blur/darken.
- **Mood:** Ominous, point of no return

### CG 4: Waking Up (Transition)

- **Description:** First-person POV from Hanna lying on her side. Everything is blurred and overexposed -- harsh surgical light from above, indistinct figures moving around her (nurses/doctors as blurry shapes). The blue curtain is visible as a wall of color to one side. A beeping monitor is a smear of green light.
- **Mood:** Disorienting, vulnerable, dread

### CG 5: Centaur Reveal (Scene 2.6)

- **Description:** The blue curtain has just fallen. Hanna looks down at herself and sees her new body -- her human torso transitions into a black horse's body where her legs used to be. Her expression is shock/horror. In the background, centaur medical staff (the Nurse and Doctor) stand naturally, as if this is completely normal to them.
- **Mood:** Body horror mixed with awe, the story's biggest visual moment

### CG 6: Meeting Aladin (Scene 2.7)

- **Description:** Hanna (centaur form) holds a newborn baby wrapped in blankets. The baby has a tuft of red hair and is tiny. A small pony body/legs peek out from the blanket wrapping. Hanna is crying but smiling -- fierce love. The clear plastic dome bassinet is nearby, lid open. Soft lighting, warm tones.
- **Mood:** Tender, triumphant, bittersweet -- the emotional payoff of the entire story

## Reference Notes for Artist

- **Centaur design:** Human upper body seamlessly attached to horse body. NOT a person riding a horse. The join should look organic/biological, like this is how they were born (because it is -- they were transformed). The Nurse and Doctor are comfortable in their bodies; Hanna is not (yet).
- **Hanna's wheelchair:** Modern/near-future military-style. Not a hoverchair (that's Mat's daughter's). Functional, no-nonsense.
- **Scale:** When centaur characters appear alongside human-form characters, the centaurs should be noticeably larger/taller due to the horse body. This is important for the power dynamic in Act 2.
- **Color palette suggestion:** Act 1 is warm golds/mahoganies (office). Transition is black. Act 2 is cool blues/whites (surgical) warming to soft amber for the final scene.
- **1001 Nights Easter eggs:** Optional -- subtle lamp/lantern motifs in the office decor, arabesque patterns in Mat's suit embroidery, etc.
