## Archive Instructions

This document receives content from VNPC_DevReference.md when:
- An **architecture pattern** is replaced -- archive the old pattern with rationale for the change
- **Coding standards** change -- archive the old standard with context on why it was updated
- **Dependencies** are removed -- archive with notes on replacement or reason for removal
- **Folder structure** undergoes major reorganization -- archive the old layout

**Do NOT archive:**
- Minor wording tweaks to standards
- Additions that don't replace existing content
